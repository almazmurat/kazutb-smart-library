Abstract

This seminal work explores the intersection of engineering excellence and environmental stewardship within the context of rapidly expanding 21st-century metropolises. Dr. Sokolova provides a comprehensive framework for analyzing urban density through the lens of ecological sustainability, offering case studies from Almaty to Oslo.

The text delves into advanced materials science, renewable energy integration at the district level, and the psychological impact of green-space architecture on urban populations. It serves as both a theoretical anchor and a practical guide for scholars of urban design and technology.
