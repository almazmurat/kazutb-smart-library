# UI Design System Demo Notes

Use the updated MVP interface as a guided progression during stakeholder review:

1. Start on the overview page to establish institutional scope and the public/secure split.
2. Move to the catalog and book details pages to show premium public discovery and availability visibility.
3. Enter the reader cabinet to demonstrate personal reservations and loans in the same visual system.
4. Continue to librarian queue and circulation to show operational control surfaces.
5. Finish in analytics and reports to reinforce management visibility and branch-aware operations.

The visual upgrade is intentionally conservative: stronger polish, stronger hierarchy, and better presentation quality without changing the underlying workflow story.
