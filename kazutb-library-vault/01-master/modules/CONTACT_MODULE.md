# CONTACT_MODULE
> Derived from [[PROJECT_CONTEXT]] §21

## Purpose
Authenticated users can send requests, complaints, suggestions, or questions to the library head, Жанерке Панкейкызы, directly through the platform.

## Submission rules
Guests cannot submit. Members, librarians, and admins can submit through a structured form linked to their corporate email identity.

## Categories
- Request
- Complaint
- Improvement suggestion
- Question
- Other

## Workflow
Messages move through `open`, `in_review`, `resolved`, and `archived`, and remain visible in librarian and admin panels.
