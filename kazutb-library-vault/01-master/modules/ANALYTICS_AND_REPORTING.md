# ANALYTICS_AND_REPORTING
> Derived from [[PROJECT_CONTEXT]] §22

## Key rule
There is no separate Analyst role. Analytics belongs inside librarian and admin functionality.

## Librarian analytics
Popular books, user activity aggregates, fund usage, circulation dynamics, monthly and annual reports, acquisition reports, and reconciliation views.

## Admin analytics
Everything available to librarians, plus system-wide activity, role distribution, integration status, repository activity, news engagement, and message-resolution tracking.

## Export requirements
Reports must export to PDF and Excel or CSV, filter by period and branch, and remain compatible with real library reporting practice.
