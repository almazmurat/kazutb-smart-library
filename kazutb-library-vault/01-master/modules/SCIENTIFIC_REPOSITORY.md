# SCIENTIFIC_REPOSITORY
> Derived from [[PROJECT_CONTEXT]] §20

## Who can access
Guests can browse metadata only. AD-authenticated users can read approved works through a controlled viewer.

## Work types
- bachelor's and diploma work
- master's dissertation
- PhD dissertation
- scientific article or paper
- research report
- university journal publication

## Metadata fields
Title, author, type, year, department, faculty, UDC, abstract, keywords, language, protected file, uploader, approver, and publication date.

## Approval model
Intake is librarian-mediated and publication is governed through [[SCIENTIFIC_REPO_WORKFLOW]].
